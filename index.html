Quinn's Breakfast Club foundation was created to bring a different style of cheer for the guests at Oshei Children's Hospital and help them power through!



Mission

With many visits to Children's Hospital and a several overnights spent there, every morning when it came to order breakfast, the picky eaters have a limited selection of what they enjoy. Not to mention from the parents perspective, we all know the negative connotation hospital food has. Depending on the time of stay at the hospital, kids tend to lose interest in the few things the hospital has to offer. That is where our foundation comes in, we have been donating small variety boxes of Cereal and activites for various children during their stay. Craft items, coloring books, crayons and markers, board games and etc. We also have been donating gift cards to the parents, to help aid them with their stay and take something smalloff their mind, such as Doordash and Uber Eats and random restaraunts for them to order and have to worry about ordering hospital food at the small time frame, or taking the hike down to the cafeteria.

What to do and how to do it!

We will gladly accept your donation and bring it to Children's hospital on your behalf. Here is a list of what we will accept and deliver. If there is a specific floor or group you would like to donate to, please let us know:

  • Money to purchase items: Cash, Check, Venmo, PayPal
  • Gift Cards to select locations: Wegmans, Tops, Walmart, Target, Amazon
  • Small Cereal Boxes, Pop Tarts, Nutrigrain bars, Granola Bars, Coffee K-Cups
  • Local and Chain restaraunt gift cards/certificates
  • Doordash, Uber Eats & Grubhub gift cards for delivery service
